About Samarth Yuva Foundation

Event Facilities reasons to give to charity

Samarth Yuva Foundation – Pune is a non-profit organization engaged in working with informal Womans, children, and Auto drivers in Pune city & other vulnerable groups in Pune.

The foundation was established in the year 2016 for doing Social welfare Activities for the urban slums and unorganized labourers. From its initiation, the foundation plays an active role in addressing the issues of domestic workers, Single mothers, Naka Workers, vulnerable communities, and other unorganized labourers.

For the past two years the foundation has organized and conducted various activities and awareness programs for the mentioned groups this includes Womans Day, Skill Development , Yoga Day by providing mental health counselling service, etc. We have recently distributed Choklates & “Khau” to various Vasti & Economically Backwards Families for Childrens. Where the Foundation is covering the expenses of Rs.1.5 Lacs.

During COVID 19 Pandemic Situation

  • 2020-2021
  • 2021-2022

Provided Dry Ration, Mask & Sanitizer Bottles.

The foundation is doing a remarkable job during in the ongoing pandemic. we were able to provide 18000 Dry Ration, 1000 Face mask & Sanitizer Bottles, 40,000 Dry snacks packets, 1500 Hygenic products, etc. for more than 75,000 people across Pune.

Impact of the Second Wave

The impact of the second wave of COVID 19 is still visiable. Despite State measures to prevent it from spreading. It is proving to be a tough task for the government. The situation tends to become more worst in the upcoming days.
